3EZWA عزوة

Referes to the concept of a close-knit group, or community that an individual is deeply connected to. This group is characterized by its strong sense of loyality and support. When someone speaks of their "عزوة" they are refereing to a group of people-often family or close allies-who will stand by then, provide protection, and support them through any challenges or dificulties. The word embodies the idea of collective strength and solidarity, where the individial finds reassurance in knowing they are not alone.
